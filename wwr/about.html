History

Since its founding, Lima's Rafting Club has been dedicated to creating unforgettable adventures on the river. Our story began with a love for nature, adrenaline, and the joy of bringing people together.

Join us as we continue to build our legacy, one rafting trip at a time. Experience the thrill, the camaraderie, and the natural beauty that inspire us daily.
